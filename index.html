Short Bio

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park, and a member of the Institute for Systems Research (ISR), since Fall 2017. I am working with John S. Baras, Distinguished University Professor and Chair in Systems Engineering, on the fields of Learning Theory, Stochastic Optimization, Systems Theory, Control and Robotics.

I have been a research intern at the Nokia Bell Labs, Murray Hill, NJ (advised by Iraj Saniee, Head, Mathematics & Algorithms Research Group), and at the Palo Alto Research Center (PARC), Palo Alto, CA (co-advised by Ion Matei and Johan de Kleer, Head, System Sciences Lab).

During my undergraduate years in ECE, NTUA, I worked as a research assistant at the Control Systems Lab (CSL) with Prof. Kostas Kyriakopoulos.
